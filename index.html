Tafseer

What is Tafseer?

Tafseer is the detailed explanation and interpretation of the Quran. It helps us understand the deeper meanings behind the verses and apply them to our lives. We offer Tafseer lessons in both Urdu and English, making it accessible for everyone.

Comprehensive Tafseer Lessons

Our Tafseer courses cover various famous works, including those by scholars like Ibn Kathir and Maulana Maududi. These lessons provide context, historical background, and practical insights into Quranic teachings.

Hadith Teaching

What is Hadith?

Hadith refers to the sayings, actions, and approvals of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). These narrations help us understand the Sunnah (practice) of the Prophet and apply it in our lives.

Famous Hadith Collections

We focus on teaching authentic Hadith from reliable collections like Sahih Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, and more. Learn how these Hadith apply to daily life.
